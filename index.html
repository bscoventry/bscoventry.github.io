About Our Lab

Our Mission

We work on cutting-edge research in neural engineering and intelligent medical devices. Our goal is to develop innovative technologies that advance our understanding of the brain and create next-generation therapeutic solutions.

Research Questions

"How can we interface with neural systems more effectively?", "What are the mechanisms underlying brain stimulation therapies?", "How can we design intelligent medical devices that adapt to individual patient needs?"

Research Philosophy

We specialize in interdisciplinary research that combines neuroscience, engineering, and clinical applications. My approach emphasizes rigorous scientific methodology, translational research, and the development of technologies that improve human health and well-being.
